Jquery Effects and Animation

Reference:
http://www.w3schools.com/jquery/jquery_ref_effects.asp
https://api.jquery.com/category/effects/

Display 3 jpg images on the screen.
Above each image put a button, which will control the image below it.

Button A: click to fadeout the image, click again to fade back in.
Button B: click to slide up the image, click agian to slide back down.
Button C: Click to move (animate()) button to left 300px, click again to restore position.
